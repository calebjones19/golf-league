How Skins Work
Each hole is worth $5. If one player has the lowest net score on a hole (relative to handicap strokes), they win the skin. If two or more players tie, the skin carries over to the next hole, making it worth more. Carried-over holes accumulate until someone wins outright.

Scoring System
Match Play: Each hole is worth 1 point. Win a hole = 1 pt, halve a hole = 0.5 pts, lose a hole = 0 pts.

Match Bonus: Win the overall match = +2 bonus points.

Handicap: Net scores used — players receive strokes on their allocated holes based on the difference in handicap versus their opponent.
